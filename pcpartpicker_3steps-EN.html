🌐 Visit pcpartpicker.com
PCPartPicker is free and you don't need an account to start. Navigate to "System Builder" or click "Start a System Build" from the homepage.
🌍 Selecting your region
Choose the country or market closest to you for realistic local prices and good parts availability.
🔧 Adding components in order
Always start with the CPU. The platform then automatically filters compatible motherboards, RAM, etc. Each component is checked for compatibility in real time.

💡 PCPartPicker runs automatic compatibility checks at every step — preventing costly mistakes.
